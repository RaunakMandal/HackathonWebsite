About Bits2Bytes

Bits2Bytes is the Official Technical Fest of Bengal Institute of Technology, Kolkata. Every Year, Students from Each Branch Participate in Various Events. This Year in Bits2Bytes, there are a Lot of Surprises waiting for you :)

Frequently Asked Questions
  1. Is the entry free?
    2. The entry has been and will always be free. All you need is a college ID!
  2. I want to be a part of organizing a team of Bits2Bytes. How can I help?
    3. You can register at College Connect Portal as the College Head (CH) or College Associate (CA) to become a part of the organizing team of Bits2Bytes.
  3. What is the artist lineup for Bits2Bytes?
    4. Stay tuned to our Official Instagram and Facebook page.
  4. Who should I contact in case of an emergency during Bits2Bytes?
  5. The emergency Contact number will be given on the registration booklet.
  6. Can people of any age-group attend Bits2Bytes?
    7. No, Only college students are allowed.
  7. Is cars allowed inside? Is parking available?
    8. No, vehicles are not allowed.
